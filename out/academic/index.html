The Four-Layer Pipeline

Why Academic mode is different from standard paraphrasing tools.

01

Term Protection

References like [12], (Smith et al., 2023) and technical terms like PCSK9, LDL-C are replaced with safe placeholders before any rewriting occurs.

02

Multi-language Rewrite

Your text travels through four languages (EN → CN → JP → FI → EN), each hop stripping out AI patterns while preserving meaning.

03

Term Restoration

After rewriting, all protected terms are restored verbatim — nothing lost, nothing invented.

04

Score Verification

The final output is checked against a public AI detector. You see the score before you submit.
